About Kavya Tours & Our Mission

Explore Comfort, Convenience, and Cost-Effective Travel

Who We Are

Awakening journey of Kavya Tours started in 2013 with a greater emphasis on “3Cs” for the passenger, i.e., Comfort, Convenience, and Cost. Initially, we began with a single bus, and over the years, due to our commitment to passenger safety, convenience, and affordability, we have expanded our fleet to 4 buses. Our services span across multiple routes and we continue to strive towards excellence in the travel and leisure sector.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, safe, and affordable travel solutions that cater to the diverse needs of our passengers. We are dedicated to enhancing the travel experience through continuous innovation, exceptional customer service, and a commitment to quality.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading travel service provider in India, known for our customer-centric approach, advanced technology integration, and sustainable practices. We aspire to expand our network and offerings to create a seamless travel experience for passengers across the nation.

More Information

We take pride in innovation and the consolidation of the bus travel industry in India. Kavya Tours introduced its first sleeper class coach along with integrated passenger, agent, and software solutions, offering a seamless travel experience.

Our mission is to extend our services across pan-India, providing a comfortable, convenient, and cost-effective travel experience for all our passengers.

Kavya Tours has become “The Choice” for passengers, agents, and the software community due to our following value-added services and offerings:

  • Availability of the latest technology and sophisticated buses ensuring utmost safety and comfort
  • Timely departures and arrivals
  • Convenience in booking, modification, and cancellation through both offline (offices, agents) and online (secure platform) modes
  • Widest routes and convenient timing for intercity bus travel
  • Affordable fares
  • Highly efficient and passenger-friendly support for query management & guidance
  • Sophisticated booking system for passenger convenience and ease
  • Wide network of offices and partners
  • Efficient disaster management in case of any breakdowns, etc.

We look forward to having you as part of our customer community for all your travel and leisure needs!

Enjoy a different world of travel with grace… with Kavya Tours.

Have a safe & comfortable journey!
